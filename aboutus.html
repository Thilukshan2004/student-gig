About StudentGig

Sri Lanka's premier job portal connecting talented students and professionals with leading companies across the nation.

Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision

StudentGig emerged from a simple idea: to bridge the gap between talented Sri Lankan students and the country's leading employers. What started as a small initiative has now grown into Sri Lanka's most trusted job portal for students and young professionals.

Our platform has helped over 15,000 students find their dream jobs and internships, partnering with more than 500 companies across various industries.
